2011–2013: Album 21 and worldwide fame


Adele released her second studio album, 21, on January 24, 2011 in the UK and on February 22 in the United States. Adele revealed that the album was inspired by a missed love affair with her ex-boyfriend. The album's rhythm has been described as classical and contemporary country and folk music. This change from the first album came from the time Adele overheard a bus driver playing contemporary music from Nashville while on tour in the American South. The album's title also points to the transformation she went through two years before the album's release. Adele told Spin magazine: "I found that interesting because I never grew up with it." 21 reached number one in 26 countries, including the UK and the US.
